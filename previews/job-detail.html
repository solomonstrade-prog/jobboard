About the role

Design, build, and operate production services end-to-end. You will own architecture decisions, mentor mid-level engineers, and partner with product on roadmap.

CloudNine Systems is a cloud-native infrastructure platform serving Fortune 500 customers. We move fast, ship often, and care deeply about reliability and developer experience.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement scalable services in Go, Node, or Python
  • Own services in production, including on-call and incident response
  • Mentor engineers and review designs for quality and consistency
  • Collaborate with product managers on roadmap and prioritization

Requirements

  • 5+ years building production systems at scale
  • Strong fundamentals in distributed systems, data modeling, and APIs
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and containers
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Nice to have

  • Open-source contributions
  • Experience with Kubernetes, Terraform, and observability tooling

Required skills
